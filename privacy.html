Privacy Policy

Last updated April 25, 2026

GymBro ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

01 Information We Collect

Data you provide directly

  • User profile (name, age, weight, height, goals)
  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight)
  • Nutrition data (meals, calories, macros)
  • Progress photos (stored locally on your device)
  • Body measurements

Data collected automatically

  • Apple HealthKit data (with your explicit permission): weight, body fat, sleep, heart rate, HRV, steps
  • Device activity data (with your permission)

Data we DO NOT collect

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not share HealthKit data with third parties

02 How We Use Your Data

  • Provide app functionality (workout, nutrition, and progress tracking)
  • Personalize training and nutrition recommendations
  • Calculate recovery and performance metrics
  • Sync data between your devices via iCloud
  • Generate AI Coach responses based on your fitness context

03 Data Storage

All your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device using SwiftData
  • In iCloud (if enabled) using CloudKit for cross-device sync
  • Progress photos are stored exclusively on your device
  • HealthKit data stays within the Apple Health ecosystem

We do not store your data on our own servers. Your information lives on your device and in your iCloud account.

04 Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit: Cross-device data sync
  • Apple HealthKit: Read/write health data (with your permission)
  • Google Gemini AI: Process AI Coach queries (queries are sent through our secure proxy and not stored)
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription processing

05 HealthKit Data

We strictly comply with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • We only access HealthKit data with your explicit consent
  • HealthKit data is used exclusively for app functionality
  • We never share HealthKit data with third parties
  • We never use HealthKit data for advertising
  • We do not store HealthKit data outside your device

06 Security

  • Sensitive data is stored in iOS Keychain
  • API communications are encrypted via HTTPS
  • API keys are never stored on the user's device
  • No data access without device authentication

07 Your Rights

  • Access: Export all your data from the app (CSV / PDF)
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data from Profile > Delete data
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats
  • Revocation: Revoke HealthKit permissions any time from iOS Settings

08 Minors

GymBro is not designed for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

09 Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
